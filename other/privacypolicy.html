This information may include your IP address, geolocation data, device identification, details about the type of browser and/or device you’re using, and the page or feature you requested. To help us recognize your browser or device and track how many people visit our Site and when, we use “cookies” which are small identifiers that we transfer to your browser or device. You may be able to adjust the settings on your browser or device to reject or limit the acceptance of cookies, but this may impact your ability to use some of our features.

        Any information that you intentionally provide to us is received and stored. For instance, if you fill out a form on the “Contact Us” page, we gather Personal Data such as your name, email address, phone number, and the company you are seeking information for. Registration with us or use of some of our features may necessitate the provision of specific information.

        If you have provided us with a means of communication, such as your email address, we may contact you. This could include promotional email offers from Electric or other businesses, or emails regarding your use of our Site. We may also receive a confirmation when you open an email from us, which helps us improve our services and make our communication with you more engaging. If you prefer not to receive any communication from us, please let us know by sending an email to contact@iab.ai.

        When you click on a link to a third-party website or service, they may also send cookies to your browser or device. You should be aware that cookies placed by third parties may still track your online activities even after you've left our Site, and those third parties may not respect your browser or device's "Do Not Track" settings.

        The data we collect from your interaction with the Site may be utilized to create customized content that we believe would be of interest to you, based on your usage patterns. Furthermore, this data can be used to enhance the Site's functionality. For instance, it enables us to understand how frequently users use specific features of the Site, allowing us to make the Site appealing to as many users as feasible.

  10. Tracking Tools, Advertising and Opt-Out

    The Site uses various tracking tools such as cookies, pixel tags, web beacons, clear GIFs, and JavaScript (collectively referred to as "Cookies") to help our servers identify your web browser, analyze your visit and usage patterns, understand trends, and improve our Site. These Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer, tablet, phone or similar device whenever you access our Site. We may also collect information from third parties, including those who have placed their own Cookies on your device(s), such as Google LLC, Marketo, 6Sense, Microsoft, Facebook, LinkedIn, and Bizible. However, please be aware that the Site does not currently support "Do Not Track" requests sent from a browser. We also offer options to opt-out of certain advertising-related Cookies, as described in the following sections.

    The following varieties of Cookies are utilized by us:

    • Essential Cookies

      These Cookies are necessary to provide you with the features or services that you have asked for. For instance, some Cookies enable you to access secure areas of our website. Disabling such Cookies may render some features and services inaccessible.

    • Performance/Analytical Cookies

      We employ these Cookies to comprehend how users interact with our Site. These Cookies gather data on the number of visitors to the Site, the pages viewed by visitors, and the duration of their visits. Additionally, Performance/Analytical Cookies help us measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns, enabling us to enhance our campaigns and Site content for individuals who engage with our ads. For instance, Google Analytics employs cookies to provide its services, and the use and sharing of data collected by Google Analytics regarding your Site visits are subject to Google's Terms of Use and Privacy Policy. If you wish to opt-out of Google's use of Cookies, you can visit the Google advertising opt-out page at www.google.com/privacy_ads.html. We also utilize Marketo, Bizible, 6Sense, LinkedIn, and Facebook to gather and analyze data about Site visitors.

    • Retargeting/Advertising Cookies

      Retargeting/Advertising Cookies amass information about your online behavior and preferences, enabling us to deliver ads that we believe are pertinent to you. To learn more about this, please refer to the section below entitled "Information about Interest-Based Advertisements." We use LinkedIn, Facebook, and 6Sense for interest-based advertising.

    You can exercise control over whether or not to accept Cookies by adjusting your internet browser's settings. Most browsers include an option for disabling the Cookie feature, which will prevent your browser from receiving new Cookies, and (depending on the capabilities of your browser software) allow you to choose to accept each new Cookie in various ways. You can also erase all existing Cookies on your device. However, if you take this action, you may need to manually configure certain preferences every time you visit our Site, and some functionalities may not operate as intended.

    To locate the Cookie settings available to you, refer to the "preferences" or "options" section of your browser's menu. To obtain additional information on Cookies, including details on how to manage and erase Cookies, please visit http://www.allaboutcookies.org/ if you are situated in the European Union.=
